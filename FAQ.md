# FAQ

## Is this a software project?

No.

It uses a software repository as a public archival form.

## Why GitHub?

Because GitHub is a place where many people expect code, history, publicness, and preservation to meet.

That expectation makes the format interesting.

## Why plain text?

Plain text is easy to copy, inspect, diff, quote, print, archive, and misunderstand without special software.

## Why bilingual?

English helps the artifact travel.

Korean preserves the origin of the thought.

## Is this finished?

It is complete enough to explain itself, but still open to refinement before it becomes a fixed artifact.
