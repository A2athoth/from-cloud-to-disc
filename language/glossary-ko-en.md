# Korean-English Glossary

A small glossary for words that carry the shape of this repository.

## 기록 / Record

A record is not only stored information.

In Korean, `기록` can also feel like the act of leaving a trace against disappearance.

## 저장소 / Repository

Literally, a place of storage.

In software, a place where changes are remembered.

## 공개 / Public

Not necessarily popular.

Not necessarily exposed.

Simply available.

## 흔적 / Trace

A sign that something passed through, even if nothing complete remains.

## 보존 / Preservation

More than keeping.

A deliberate refusal to let something vanish too easily.

## 물성 / Materiality

The quality of being a physical thing.

A disc gives a digital record a temporary sense of weight.

## 아카이브 / Archive

Not only storage, but a promise that something may be found again.
