# Almost Untranslatable Words

Some words can be translated, but not completely moved.

## 흔적

A trace, but softer than evidence.

A sign that something or someone passed through, even if nothing complete remains.

## 아깝다

A feeling that something still had value, even if it was not fully used, kept, or understood.

## 정

A slow emotional residue between people, places, and time.

## 여운

The remaining vibration after an event, sentence, sound, or meeting has ended.

## 기록

A record, but also an action.

Not just what remains, but the decision to let something remain.
