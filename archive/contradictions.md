# Contradictions

This archive preserves a few contradictions instead of resolving them.

## Digital and physical

The repository is digital, but it imagines becoming physical.

## Public and quiet

The repository is public, but it does not ask to be popular.

## Small and complete

The repository is small, but it tries to be complete enough to explain itself.

## English and Korean

The repository uses English to travel and Korean to remember where it began.

## Temporary and preserved

The event may be temporary. The record may last longer than the event.
