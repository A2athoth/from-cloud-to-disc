# Predictions

These predictions are allowed to be wrong.

## 2030

- Most people will use personal AI agents daily.
- Keyboards will still exist.
- Email will still refuse to die.
- Plain text will remain useful.
- Some people will miss software that did less.
- More work will be reviewed through generated summaries before anyone reads the original.
- The word “cloud” will feel older than the systems it describes.

## 2040

- Some archives will be valuable because they are small enough to understand.
- People will still rediscover the value of local files.
- Many digital memories will survive in formats no one expected to matter.
- A few compact discs will still be readable, and many links will not.
