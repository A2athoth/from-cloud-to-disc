# Things That May Age Strangely

Some ordinary things from this moment may look strange later.

- People still typed passwords manually.
- People asked whether text was written by AI.
- People copied error messages into search engines.
- People stored important work inside chat platforms.
- People called remote computers “the cloud.”
- People treated screenshots as evidence and then lost the context around them.
- People subscribed to tools they did not fully trust.
- People worried that machines might sound too human.
- People used physical metaphors for almost everything digital: folders, files, windows, desktops, trash.
- People still argued about whether a repository without code was a real repository.
