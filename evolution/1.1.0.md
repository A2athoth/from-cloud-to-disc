# Evolution Note: 1.1.0

## Repository state reviewed

At 1.0.1, the archive had reached a coherent candidate-complete shape, but continued growth risked becoming mere accumulation.

## Reconsideration

Before adding more material, the archive needed a curation model: a way to decide what belongs and what should be left out.

## Changes made

- Added a curation folder.
- Added principles, non-goals, and selection criteria.
- Reframed future growth as selection rather than expansion for its own sake.

## Result

The archive can continue growing without losing its shape as easily.
