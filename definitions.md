# Definitions

Small definitions for ordinary digital words.

## Repository

A place where changes are allowed to have memory.

## Commit

A small proof that a particular version existed.

## Branch

A possible future that has not yet been merged.

## Merge

The act of deciding that two histories can become one.

## README

A door left open for a stranger.

## Public

Visible to everyone, even if almost no one looks.

## Private

Not necessarily secret. Sometimes simply unfinished.

## Archive

A message sent without knowing who will receive it.

## Backup

A promise made before panic.

## Log

A system's memory of what it thinks happened.

## Cloud

Someone else's computer, treated like part of our memory.

## Disc

A physical circle that makes digital things feel briefly mortal.

## Link

A path that may or may not still exist later.

## File

A container for a thought that wanted a name.

## Folder

A room built out of names.

## Terminal

A place where humans still speak to machines in short, precise sentences.

## Error

A machine asking for a better explanation.

## Version

A way to say: this was true then.

## License

A statement about what others may do after the author stops paying attention.

## Preservation

The practice of giving the future something to misunderstand carefully.
